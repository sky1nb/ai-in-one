# Privacy Policy for AI in One

**Last Updated:** January 27, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to **AI in One** ("we," "our," or "the app"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AI in One handles data when you use our desktop application.

**AI in One does NOT collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.**

### What We DON'T Collect:
- ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone)
- ❌ Usage analytics or telemetry
- ❌ Browsing history or conversation data

- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Any form of tracking data

## Local Data Storage

### Cookies and Authentication
- AI in One stores **authentication cookies locally** on your device for convenience
- These cookies are used solely to maintain your login sessions with AI services
- Cookies are stored **only on your computer** and are never transmitted to our servers
- You can clear cookies at any time through the app's settings

### Session Data
- Session information (login states) is stored **locally in your device's storage**
- This data never leaves your computer
- We do not have access to your session data

---

## Data Transmission

**AI in One does NOT operate any backend servers.**

- All communication happens **directly between your device and the AI services**
- We do not act as a proxy or intermediary
- No data passes through our infrastructure
- The app functions as a secure container for accessing AI platforms

---

## Security

While we do not collect data, we implement security best practices:

- **Isolated Sessions:** Each AI service runs in an isolated environment
- **Local Storage:** All data is stored securely on your device
- **No External Tracking:** No third-party analytics or tracking scripts
- **Regular Updates:** We provide security updates through Microsoft Store

---

## Children's Privacy

AI in One does not knowingly collect any data from children. However, we recommend:
- **Parental guidance** for users under 13
- Review the privacy policies of individual AI services before allowing children to use them
- Some AI services have age restrictions (typically 13+)
